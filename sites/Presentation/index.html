What is Pedal PDX?

Pedal PDX is an android application developed by the Team of 7 student at Portland State University as part of Computer Science Senior Capstone during Fall 2013 - WInter 2014. Pedal PDX helps you with your bike ride. So it is also known as "A cyclist's best friend!". You could use the app to track your bike route which is accompained by timer which helps you track yout bike time. Later after your ride you will see your ride stats such as Time, Miles and for the fun, no of burritos burned by converting the calories. You will also be able to view your route in the Maps. But don't worry. We care about your privacy. You can direct the app such as when to start tracking and when to stop.



We hope you enjoy the app and if you have not already please download it form the Play Store.
